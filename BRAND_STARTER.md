# Sports Analytics Brand Starter

## Brand Position

Build the company around a simple promise:

**clear betting intelligence for people who want an edge without guessing.**

The parent brand should stay sport-agnostic. It should feel like a trusted analytics company that can cover MLB, NFL, NBA, and future markets without sounding tied to one season or one sport.

The first product can be MLB-focused, but the company name should not use baseball language. Baseball-specific identity belongs at the product level.

## Brand Pillars

- **Transparent edge:** Show the logic behind every pick. Do not present plays as magic.
- **Fast slate clarity:** Help members identify the few players worth attention today.
- **Source-aware confidence:** Lineups, weather, pitcher matchups, and market signals should be visible.
- **Disciplined betting voice:** Smart, calm, sharp, and confident without sounding reckless.

## Audience

The first audience is daily sports bettors who want better process, not loud picks. They care about HR props, parlays, player props, odds movement, and late lineup changes. They likely follow X accounts for picks, but they will pay for a dashboard if it saves time and feels trustworthy.

## Brand Architecture

Recommended structure:

- Parent company: broad sports analytics brand
- Product family: sport-specific tools
- Member dashboard: one paid hub with separate product areas

Example:

- Parent brand: **Slate Signal**
- MLB product: **BarrelIQ**
- NFL product: **RedZoneIQ**
- NBA product: **CourtIQ**

This keeps the company expandable while letting each product speak the language of its sport.

## Visual Direction

The brand should feel like a premium betting terminal, not a hype page.

Core style:

- dark interface base
- clean data tables
- sharp accent color
- badge system for signals
- restrained sports-energy visuals
- no cartoon mascots
- no casino look

Suggested palette:

- Ink: `#0B1115`
- Charcoal: `#111A20`
- Signal Green: `#22C55E`
- Warning Gold: `#F5C84C`
- Heat Red: `#F04438`
- Ice Blue: `#38BDF8`
- Text: `#E6EEF2`
- Muted Text: `#8EA0AA`

Avoid making the brand only red/orange because that can feel too much like generic betting tout content.

## Member Dashboard Roadmap

Phase 1:

- Brand the existing local Hitter Tool dashboard as **BarrelIQ** inside the broader parent brand.
- Add a clean header, logo wordmark, member-style navigation, and daily slate date.
- Improve filters for Top HR, Weather Edge, Pitcher Target, Sleeper, and Watch.
- Add disclaimer and source status.

Phase 2:

- Add password-protected member access.
- Store daily reports by date.
- Add result tracking and hit-rate review.
- Add CSV/Excel downloads.

Phase 3:

- Add odds and value-gap layer.
- Add user accounts and Stripe subscriptions.
- Add NBA/NFL product areas.
